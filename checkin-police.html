🏛️ What is this?
Every host in the Balearic Islands is required to present a list of all guests with their identifications within 24h after the Check-in. (Law 1/1992, of 21 February, INT/1922/2003, 3 July). As it is sanctioned with heavy fines, we must deliver the report in time. We have prepared this tool so you can submit your data before your holidays and relax when you arrive!
The information is only stored in the database of the Spanish police and not used for any other purposes. Please use the link below for each registration of all guests.
